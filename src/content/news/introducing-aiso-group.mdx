Four specialist AI companies are now one group. Here is why we built it this way, what each brand does, and what it is not.

## The short version

AISO Group is the parent of four specialist AI companies, each with one job:

- **[AISO Hub](https://aiso-hub.com)** delivers [AI search optimization](/brands/aiso-hub/) - making your brand the answer AI cites.
- **[AISO Dev](https://aiso-dev.com)** builds [AI systems for SMBs](/brands/aiso-dev/) your team can run.
- **[AISO Buzz](https://aiso-buzz.com)** produces [AI-powered social and content](/brands/aiso-buzz/) that sounds like you, not like AI.
- **[AISO Learn](https://aiso-learn.com)** delivers [corporate AI training](/brands/aiso-learn/), from AI-curious to AI-capable.

Each company has its own site, its own team, its own voice. The group exists for one reason: small and mid-sized businesses deserve specialists, not generalists.

## Why a group, not an agency

Generalist AI agencies have a structural problem. The work spreads thin. The team optimizes for retainer hours, not outcomes. The client ends up paying for a bundle they never needed.

A specialist team ships faster, cheaper, and sharper - but only on one thing. The group model keeps the specialists specialist, and gives the client a way to add the next one when the work is ready.

## The verb test

Every sibling earns its place on the roster by passing a verb test:

- **[Hub cites](/brands/aiso-hub/).** Content architected to be cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews.
- **[Dev builds](/brands/aiso-dev/).** AI systems - agent workflows, data pipelines, internal tools - with a human in the loop.
- **[Buzz says](/brands/aiso-buzz/).** Social and content produced from a brand bible, validated before it ships.
- **[Learn teaches](/brands/aiso-learn/).** Corporate AI training with EU AI Act-aligned curricula - proof of competence, not a PDF.

If a piece of work does not pass the verb test for its brand, it does not belong there.

## What the group itself does

The group does not deliver. The group routes.

- We triage inbound on [aiso-group.com/contact](/contact/).
- We maintain the shared standards - human review on every AI output, brand-first process, scoped work.
- We handle cross-referrals between brands without running a second sales cycle.

If you already know which specialist you need, go directly to the brand. If you do not, we route you in one conversation.

## Who this is for

EU-based small and mid-sized businesses with a real problem and a real budget. Not venture-funded experiments. Not enterprise procurement cycles. Real businesses with real customers.
